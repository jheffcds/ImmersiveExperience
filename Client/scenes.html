Starry Night

Story: Painted by Vincent van Gogh in 1889 while in an asylum at Saint-Rémy-de-Provence, Starry Night is one of the most famous works of art in the world. It captures the swirling energy of the night sky over a sleepy village.

Description: The scene blends imagination with observation — the large cypress tree connects the land and sky, while the whirling stars evoke a sense of cosmic movement and emotion.
